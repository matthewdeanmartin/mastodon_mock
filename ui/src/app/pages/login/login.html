How do I choose a server?
  • Some servers are special-interest communities (tech, art, a language…). Their local feed reads like a forum for that topic — a nice built-in home timeline.
  • Small servers see less of the network: if you follow hashtags, expect fewer results than on a big server.
  • Small servers run on donations and volunteer time, so they can be less financially stable in the long run.
  • You can move your account (and followers) to another server later.
